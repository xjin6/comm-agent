## Section A — AI Chatbot Usage Patterns (all 1–5 unless noted)

### A1: General AI usage frequency (1=几个月1次 … 6=每天)
Single item.

### A2: Specific chatbot usage (1=从不 … 5=几乎每天)
A2r1=DeepSeek, A2r2=豆包, A2r3=ChatGPT, A2r4=文心一言, A2r5=Claude, A2r6=Gemini, A2r7=Copilot, A2r8=其他

### A3: Reasons for AI use — 3 sub-dimensions (1=非常不同意 … 5=非常同意)
- **Utility/information** (A3r1–r6): fast info, knowledge, answers, efficiency, time-saving, work/study assistance
- **Entertainment** (A3r7–r9): fun, novelty, relaxation
- **Social/emotional** (A3r10–r15): social experience, responsiveness, companionship, stress relief, emotional listening, feeling understood

### A4: AI FOMO — Fear of missing out on AI (3 items, 1–5)
A4r1–r3: others gain more, friends gain more, work efficiency will suffer

### A5: Perceived AI deception risk (13 items, 1–5)
- **Intent** (A5r1–r4): mislead public, influence judgment, alter behavior, benefit manipulators
- **Detectability** (A5r5–r7): hard to detect, hides mechanisms, mimics real content
- **Consequences** (A5r8–r13): misconceptions, false beliefs, financial loss, privacy breach, reputation damage, decision errors

### A6: AI chatbot habit/integration (5 items, 1–5)
Daily activity integration, willingness to mention to others, habitual use, fear of falling behind, regret if unavailable

### A7: Algorithmic awareness (13 items, 1=完全不了解 … 5=非常了解)
- **Personalization awareness** (A7r1–r10): algorithm recommends, prioritizes, personalizes, differentiates users, automates decisions, uses behavioral data
- **Transparency/privacy concerns** (A7r11–r13): lack of transparency, human bias, privacy impact of personalization

### A8: Objective AI knowledge (5 quiz items, 1–4 each)
Correct answers: A8_1=1, A8_2=1, A8_3=4, A8_4=2, A8_5=3

### A9: AI literacy / competence (12 items, 1–5)
- **Knowledge & skills** (A9r1–r6): identify AI devices, understand AI, recognize AI in apps, proficient use, ease of learning, productivity
- **Critical evaluation** (A9r7–r9): assess capabilities, compare solutions, select appropriate tools
- **Ethical awareness** (A9r10–r12): ethics compliance, privacy/security awareness, vigilance against misuse

### A10: Perceived AI sycophancy (9 items, 1–5)
AI agrees regardless, treats input as fact, adjusts to user opinion, says what user wants to hear, overly positive, seeks approval, rarely corrects user, avoids disagreement, reluctant to say "you may be wrong"

### A11: Deepfake exposure/concern (3 items, 1–5)
Frequency of encountering deepfakes, severity of being deceived, impact on decision-making

### A12: AI hallucination concern (3 items, 1–5)
Encounters false-but-confident answers, serious impact if used unchecked, need for high vigilance

### A13: Privacy concerns with AI (4 items, 1–5)
Data breach risk, general privacy worry, misuse by third parties, unanticipated data use

### A14: Aggression toward AI (7 items, 1–5)
Insults when AI is stubborn, scolds AI for refusal, verbal abuse to "correct" AI, loses patience, mocks AI's stupid answers, uses strong language to force compliance, verbal attacks when cannot rebut

### A15: Self-disclosure to AI (4 items, 1–5) — note: r4–r7 in data (r1–r3 excluded)
Fully and honestly disclose self, unintentionally disclose, complete self-disclosure when chatting, disclosure persists long

### A16: Perceived AI performance quality (5 items, 1–5)
Timely response, accurate/reliable info, context coherence, varied responses, broad knowledge

### A17: AI satisfaction (4 items, 1–5)
Satisfied, interesting, fulfilling, happy

### A18: Trust in AI (3 items, 1–5)
AI genuinely cares, provides accurate info, fulfills responsibilities

### A19: Social sharing about AI (4 items, 1–5)
Share via WeChat, discuss on social media, repost AI-related content, recommend AI on social profile
